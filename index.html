Places to Go

There are several places to go in Alaska and several options on how to travel to Alaska. I'm going to focus on Cruising to Alaska.

Ketchikan

Ketchikan is one of several ports that you can stop at and is a beautiful place rainforest. Yep a rainforest in Alaska!!!

Juneau

This is actually the capitol of Alaska because back during the gold rush this is where all the banks were.

Skagway

Skagway is a very tiny town of only 850 people in the off season of cruising.

Glaciers

There are several glaciers that be seen from the ship or you can take a private tour.
